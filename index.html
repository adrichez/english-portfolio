About Me

Originally from Beas de Segura (Jaén) 🌳, I'm passionate about learning, exploring new ideas, and connecting with people from different backgrounds. Over the years, I've balanced my studies with work in hospitality, agriculture, and education 🍽️.

I hold a BSc in Statistics from the University of Granada 🎓. During my degree, I studied abroad at the Università di Padova (Italy) 🇮🇹 and was selected among the top 20 students for the UGR Talent Incubator’s leadership program 🎖️. These experiences, along with early cultural exchanges in England 🇬🇧, helped me grow immensely both personally and professionally.

In 2023/2024, I took a gap year to volunteer with the European Solidarity Corps 🤲, take courses, and work abroad. This period allowed me to gain new skills, build meaningful relationships, and improve my languages 🇪🇺.

I recently completed my MSc in Data Science and Computer Engineering 👨🏻‍💻🧬. Alongside my studies, I worked as a Bioinformatics Developer at CSIC-IPBLN 🧫, where I developed my Master's thesis (GenoScribe) and combined data science with cutting-edge biomedical research.

Currently, I am working as an R&D&I Technical Support and Management Specialist at the Institute of Mathematics of the University of Granada (IMAG) 🖥️. In this role, I manage IT infrastructures, administer web systems, and provide direct technical support to research groups, continuing my journey at the intersection of technology, data, and science!
